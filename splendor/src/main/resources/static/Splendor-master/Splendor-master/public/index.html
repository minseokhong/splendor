Game Information
  • If you've never played, or just need a refresher on the rules, look here!
  • To reserve a card, hold shift while clicking on the card.
  • To take two tokens at once, hold shift and click on the tokens. Alternatively, you can simply click on the token stack twice.
  • If you're a player and you disconnect, the game will end for everyone else. So don't do that. Don't be a quitter.
